How to play

1. Click on any picture to select a character. A star will appear on the chosen character to indicate your selection. To remove the star, simply refresh the page.

2. Ask your opponent a yes or no question to help guess their character.

3. Click the blue door next to the image to eliminate the wrong characters. You can reopen the door by clicking it again.

4. Guess your opponents charater right before they do

5. If you guess a charater wrong your opponent wins the game
